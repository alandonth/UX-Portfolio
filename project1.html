What'sHapp

A mobile app made for those who need a little spontaneity in their travel planning.

Responsibilities

UX Researcher, UX Designer

Deliverables

Persona, Journey Map, User Flow, Wireframes, Prototype

Research

Our research began by conducting face to face interviews in search of qualitative data as well as distributing a survey for more quantitative data. We asked questions that gave us valuable insight into the mindset of modern travelers. After we listened, we began to analyze and synthesize our data utilizing our user insight board, affinity diagram, and empathy map. It wasn't long before we discovered that our users weren't looking for a way to plan their vacations but a way to feel confident in booking a vacation without losing the freedom and flxibility of being spontaneous.

"I'm the type of person that can plan out 60% of the trip and purposely leave 40% of it unplanned to be spontaneous. " -Vishal (interviewee)

Prototype

After we defined the problem we were solving for we ideated and converged on a single idea that we felt was ready to be mocked up. We started by creating paper sketches that were quickly followed by Lo-Fi digital mockups. We then took the prototype to users to test and iterate upon. After conducting guerrilla user tests, the insights gathered were organized into 4 main categories and grouped based on the frequency of responses. We took these insights and iterated on our prototype.

Solution

After analyzing and iterating on testing results we came up with a solution that allowed users to create vacation plans in minimal time while maintaining a sense of freedom and spontaneity. We call it What'sHapp, a mobile app that gets it's name from the users mindset of exploring the area they are in. We hope you like it.
